About

The objective of the project is to delineate, map and benchmark the coastal hazard line all along the mainland coast of Maharashtra.
At present Erosion Control Measures works are proposed in state budget as per the demand of local villagers, representative of state (MLA & MP), local authorities such as Grampanchayat & fisheries society etc. As and when demanded the Erosion Control Measures works are considered as per availability of fund. Due to non availability of Vulnerability index, Department is not able to decided upon priority of works on technical grounds instead it is decided upon the demand & urgency on that time. In view of the above circumstances a thorough survey of coastline with latest technology is required for preparing Coastal Management Plan for erosion control measures to ensure effective protection of shore lines, coastal infrastructure, livelihoods and lives.
Under this program, fixing the benchmark all along the Maharashtra Coastline, collecting the ground truth information with detailed topography survey, generating coastal vulnerability map for erosion and finally develop and establish the web database server to upload and save generated results and data for further sharing and monitoring purpose

Project Brief


The main scope of work includes establishing the BM all along the Maharashtra coastline of around 720 km at every 10 km with an chainage of 500 meter. All the BM and chainage points should be marked with standard dimension stone. Carry out the drone topography lidar survey / Aerial Topography lidar survey with a GSD less than 5 cm of ground area / ground and accuracy of 5 c only in all directions / with an accuracy of less than 5 cm. in all X-Y-Z direction of entire 720 km Maharashtra coastline with a width of approx. 800 to 1000 meter depending on the coast. Data will be collected in such a way that waterline should be clearly visible.
Date and time period should be recorded at the time data collection for estimation of tidal level. Generation of Orthomosaic image and Point cloud data will be highly accurate with respect to MSL. The point cloud generated from the lidar should be RGB enable and all the point cloud should be required classified as per standard class defined by authority, multibeam bathymetry survey using USV is necessary to identify the bed of sea shore to Identification of all coastal structure like â€“ sea wall, groins etc. which effect the coastal vulnerability. All the data will be generated as per the taluka wise existing protection structure.

Generate the coastal erosion & accretion vulnerability map of entire Maharashtra coastline. Consider both environmental and social parameter to estimate vulnerability. Estimate the long term and short-term erosion pattern starting from 1990 to present. Result will be represented in term taluka, district and state wise vulnerability map generated over Drone captured image.

Work Flow

1.Fixing Benchmark and establishing coastal chainage

2.Drone Topography lidar survey /Aerial Topography lidar survey

3.Multibeam Bathymetry using USV

4.Vulnerability map generation

5.Web base portal and mobile app development with hardware server setup
